What we do

With two decades of experience in the biological sciences, Dexter Biometry can provide expert assistance to help you analyze a wide variety of data types, including

  • Transcriptomics
  • Proteomics
  • TCR/BCR Sequencing
  • Survival Experiments
In addition to analysis projects, we also offer help with
  • Fully custom applications to visualize and explore your data
  • Manuscript-ready figure generation
  • Process Automation
  • Cleaning up existing pipelines
  • Scaling pipelines locally or on the cloud
  • Efficient biological data storage
  • Databases
  • Public Data Mining
Please contact us to find out how Dexter Biometry can help design and perform a custom analysis for your project.
